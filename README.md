# 💫 About Me:
I don’t just learn tech I try to understand why systems fail and how to make them impossible to ignore.<br><br>I’m currently building my foundation in Generative AI and backend systems, but what I really focus on is decision making systems ; the kind that reduce chaos, not just process it.<br><br>For example, I recently worked on a task allocation system that doesn’t just assign work - it questions whether the work should exist in the first place.<br><br>Long term, I’m aiming to build products at the intersection of AI and real-world utility - not hype-driven tools, but systems people actually depend on.<br><br>Right now, I’m looking to learn from people who are building things that matter - and contribute wherever I can add real value.<br>
